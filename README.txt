== Description ==

WebMaxy Performance Marketing is an all-in-one eCommerce customer management platform that gives you direct access to all your customer data and interactions in one place. It integrates your data and tech stack seamlessly and begins to produce marketing reports. Analyze your different marketing campaigns to learn about your customers’ clicks, impressions, and campaign spending. Examine your marketing channels through different attribution models and make smart investments.
Features 

1) Customer Data Platform 
2) Marketing Analytics 
3) Email campaigns and workflow 
4) Loyalty points and rewards
 
= Key benefits = 

1) Conversion Dashboard 
   Analyze your conversions of organic & paid marketing channels and their revenue contribution. Make data-driven decisions & smart investments.

2) Audience Segmentation 
   Segment your customer data to personalize your marketing campaigns. Target customers based on their recent actions and buying journey stage.

3) Automate Workflows
   Create customer journey workflows that make customers’ navigation easy and seamless. Set automated email campaigns to persuade customers.
